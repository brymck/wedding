We were able to get a deal with the Marriott in Charlotte near our house for $94 (one king) or $104 (two queens) per night. If you mention the McKelvey wedding, they will honor this rate until March 28th. It's in a nice place with good amenities.

Generally, we recommend staying near our house (9427 Penshurst Trace, Charlotte, NC). Hotels near the airport are few and distant from the venue. Hotels in the city are also a bit distant, although uptown is a nice area as well to spend down time.
